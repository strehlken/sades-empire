The Gallery

Gallery. Showroom.
Incubator.

Ground Objects is a trade and retail gallery for collectible design — emerging studio makers, limited-edition commissions, and curated vintage chosen for material integrity and contemporary relevance. We find the next generation of American ceramicists, glassblowers, and furniture makers before the market does, and bring their work to the most design-literate community in the Northeast.
